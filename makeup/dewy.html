DEWY MAKEUP

A dewy makeup look is one that gives the skin the appearance of a hydrated look with radiance to it. It exudes a healthy-looking skin that is soft, moist, and youthful glow that comes from within. The dewy look is not to be confused with glitter or shimmering skin which assumes a skin that is not natural to the skin.

Apply A Primer

Applying Foundation

ADVANTAGES

1.Natural Glow

Dewy makeup mimics the natural glow of healthy, well-hydrated skin. It provides a luminous finish that looks youthful and radiant, contributing to a fresh and healthy appearance.

2.Hydration

Dewy makeup often includes products that are formulated with moisturizing ingredients. This can be beneficial for individuals with dry or dehydrated skin, as the makeup helps maintain skin hydration throughout the day.

3.Youthful Appearance

The dewy finish can give the illusion of youthfulness by creating a soft, luminous glow. It tends to reflect light, which can minimize the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, contributing to a more youthful complexion.

4.Healthy Look

The radiant finish of dewy makeup can make the skin look healthy and vibrant. It's an excellent choice for individuals who want to achieve a lit-from-within glow and showcase a naturally healthy complexion.

5.Soft Focus

Dewy makeup tends to create a soft focus effect on the skin, diffusing light and blurring imperfections. This can result in a more even skin tone and a smoother overall appearance.
