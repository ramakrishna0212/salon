ADVANTAGES

1.Natural Ingredients

Mineral makeup is typically formulated with natural minerals such as zinc oxide, titanium dioxide, and iron oxides. These ingredients are often gentle on the skin and may be suitable for individuals with sensitive skin or those prone to allergies.

2.No Harsh Chemicals

Mineral makeup formulations often exclude common irritants and harsh chemicals, such as parabens, synthetic fragrances, preservatives, and fillers. This makes it a preferable option for those who want to minimize their exposure to potentially harmful substances.

3.Sun Protection

Many mineral makeup products contain ingredients like zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, which offer some degree of sun protection by acting as physical barriers against UV rays.
