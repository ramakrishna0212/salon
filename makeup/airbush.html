AIRBUSH MAKEUP

Airbrush makeup is makeup sprayed onto the skin using an airbrush machine instead of being applied with sponges, brushes, fingers, or other methods. A typical airbrush system uses a compressor to create airflow through a hose connected to a trigger-controlled spray painting gun.

TECHNIQUES

ADVANTAGES

1.Flawless Finish

Airbrush makeup provides a seamless and flawless finish on the skin. The fine mist of makeup creates a smooth appearance, covering imperfections, blemishes, and uneven skin tone.

2.Buildable Coverage

Airbrush makeup allows for buildable coverage, meaning you can layer the product to achieve the desired level of coverage. This makes it versatile for both light and heavy makeup looks.

3.Lightweight

Airbrush makeup is known for its lightweight feel. The formula is typically very thin, so it doesn't feel heavy on the skin. This is especially beneficial.
